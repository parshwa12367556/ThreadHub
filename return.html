Return & Exchange Policy

We want you to be happy with your purchase. If you're not satisfied, here are the details on how to return or exchange items.

Eligibility

Items must be returned within 30 days of delivery in their original condition: unworn, unwashed, with tags attached, and in original packaging.

How to Start a Return

  1. Log in to your account and open your Orders page.
  2. Select the order and item(s) you want to return and choose a reason.
  3. Print the return label (or follow instructions for carrier drop-off).

Refunds & Exchanges

Once we receive and inspect the return, we'll process refunds or help with exchanges within 5-10 business days.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, contact us at fabricfashion@gmail.com with photos and your order number.
